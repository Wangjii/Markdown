Markdown Basic Grammar
=====
1. ## Title
   # First
   ## Second
   ### Third 
   #### fourth
   ##### fifth
   ###### sixth
   ---
   also first
   ==========
   also second
   -------
0. ## Paragraphs
   
   1. ### To generate multiple paragraghs(create two &lt;p&gt;)
      > #### add an empty line between each paragragh
   0. ### A newline in a paragragh(only create one &lt;p&gt;)
      > #### add two spaces at the end of the last line      
0. ## Quote
    >### use ">"   
    ### For example  
    >### This is a quote
0. ## Split line
    > ### use three consecutive asterisk（*）、minus（-）、baseline（_）;and you can add space in the middle
    ### For example
   '---'
  
   ---   <!--to add a blank line to the two level title-->
   '- - -'
   - - -
   '***'
   ***
   '* * *'
   * * *
   '___'
   ___
   '_ _ _'
   _ _ _   
0. ## Emphasize   
   > ### use single asterisk to ltalic ;and use double asterisk to emphasize
    ### For example
   <em>ltalic</em> <strong>emphasize</strong>   
   *single*     _single_  
   **double**   __double__
0. ## List
    ### list symbol：  
   1. ### unordered list
      - “-”
      * “*”
      + “+”
   0. ### ordered list  
      ### number “1. 2. 0. ”
0. ## Hyperlink  
    - ### https://wwww.baidu.com
    - ###  <https://www.baidu.com>
    - ### [Baidu](https://www.baidu.com)
    - ### [Baidu](https://www.baidu.com "Baidu")  
    - ### [hyperlink text](url(href) “title”)
0. ## Code highlighting   
   * ### expression one `
   * ### expression two ~  
   
     ### For example
      ~~~ python
      print("Hello world")
      ~~~
0. ## Image  
   >### ![alt text](image's url image's title)
     ### For example  
     ### ![This is a picture](url "picture")
0. ## Tabel display  
   > ### use - or |   
    ### For example
     
     >|TH1|TH2|TH3|
     >----|----|----|
     >|TD1|TD3|TH5|
     >|TD2|TD4|TD6|  
     ---
     >| left | center | right |
     >|:---|:---:|---:|
     >|1 |2 |3 |
     >|4 |5 |6 |
